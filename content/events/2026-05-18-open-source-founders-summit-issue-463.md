Open Source Founders Summit is a business conference, not a tech conference. Open source companies face challenges and opportunities that proprietary software companies never encounter. General startup programs, investors and your own employees often won’t understand how to navigate these challenges or take advantage of the opportunities unless they have open source experience. Open Source Founders Summit gives founders a place to learn from each other and support each other in building open source companies.
